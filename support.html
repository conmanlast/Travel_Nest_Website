Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a destination? +

You can save destinations by using the "Add to Wishlist" button on the Random Trip Generator page!

Is TravelNest free to use? +

Yes, TravelNest is completely free for all students and families.

How does the budget planner work? +

Simply enter your destination, the number of days, and your daily budget. We'll calculate the total and categorize your spending level.
